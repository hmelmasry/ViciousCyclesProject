About us

Vicious Cycles is a local store for Bikers located in Cary,NC.It is a based cycle workshop founded by a few like-minded individuals in late 2010.

Vicious Cycles build and sell top quality cycling equipment.We sell motorcycles, SYM scooters and all accessories needed for bikers.

The motorcycles available at the store are :

  • Kawasaki
  • Harley Davidson
  • American Eagle

Our mechanics build the bikes and man the workshop. We hope to create and foster a strong passion for the local biking scene because each and every one of us is a biking enthusiast.

We believe that every machine should be an extension of its owner.We believe in the stories between your cycle and you. We believe in having fun. No matter the circumstances, we bike hard and we bike often.

You get on your bike, you ride, you fall, you might get pulled over, you overcome it all and you ride again. It’s a Vicious Cycle! So make it worth the while, don't just be part of the numbers.

Ride your cycle, chase your dreams, live your life.
